Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 7-day return policy work? +
You can return your vehicle within 7 days of delivery for a full refund, no questions asked. The vehicle must be in the same condition as delivered with less than 250 miles added. Simply schedule a pickup through your account and we'll handle the rest.
What is included in the 100-day warranty? +
Our 100-day warranty covers all major mechanical and electrical components including engine, transmission, drivetrain, and more. See our warranty page for complete coverage details.
How long does delivery take? +
Most vehicles are delivered within 3-7 business days depending on your location. You can track your delivery in real-time through your account.
Can I get pre-approved for financing? +
Yes! You can get pre-approved in minutes with no impact to your credit score. Our financing rates start at 4.99% APR with terms up to 84 months.
How do I sell my car to ZoomLoop? +
Get an instant offer by entering your vehicle details on our Sell page. If you accept, we'll schedule a free pickup at your convenience and pay you the same day.
